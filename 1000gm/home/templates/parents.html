For Parents

Here are the benefits of contributing to 1000 GM for your kids’ chess journey:

  • • Your donation qualifies as a charitable contribution and is eligible for company match.
  • • Participating in 30 Scheveningen Challenger tournamnets a year can help your kids manage opponent ratings and achieve higher ratings.
  • • These tournaments are cost-effective and the best way to maximize ratings.
  • • Donating $30,000 to 1000 GM is equivalent to $15000 of post-tax money and can cover 10-15 big Swiss tournamnets with all expenses included.
  • • Your kids’ games will be broadcasted live on chess.com using DGT Boards.
  • • Share your story on 1000 GMs YouTube channel and join monthly Zoom calls to co-create the tournament experience.
  • • 1000 GM is dedicated to improving the chess parents experience.
  • • Join the 1000 GM community to help your kids achieve their chess dreams with a fulfilling and cost-effective tournament experience.
