For GM's

We offer attractive conditions for GMs to play in GM Norm and Scheveningen Challenger Tournaments, with the potential to make a full-time job out of playing in 4 such Tournaments a month.

We have paid more than 30 GMs to play in our tournaments. To get corporate sponsorship, professional chess needs a paying fan base. We plan to create new tournaments that encourage audience interaction, though they may not be FIDE-rated.

We have player interviews on the 1000 GM YouTube channel. GMs can give back by volunteering to play in one of our tournamnets.
